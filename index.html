Draw your own pattern

Here, you can input your own pattern. In the box, enter any combination of "F", "-", or "+" symbols to "draw forward", "turn right 60 degrees", or "turn left 60 degrees" respectively. This follows the Lindenmayer system. The default to create a Koch Snowflake fractal is "f-f++f-f".
